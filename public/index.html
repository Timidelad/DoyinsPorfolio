ABOUT
OLUMUYIWA, an enthusiastic, dedicated, and creative writer, sees writing as a calling. She finds joy in holding a pen and believes her outstanding creativity is evident through her works. However, writing is a continuous learning process for her. While currently earning a degree in Law, Olu also enjoys other hobbies, such as reading, dancing, listening to good music, and learning. For Olu, writing is one of the places where she finds true happiness, and this is what she hopes for her readers too.
