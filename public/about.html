ABOUT ME
OLU ADEDOYIN ALUKO, an enthusiastic, dedicated, and creative writer, sees writing as a calling. She finds joy in holding a pen and believes her outstanding creativity can be vouched for through her works. However, Olu doesn't consider her works perfect, as she keeps improving and nurturing her writing talent. While pursuing an ongoing degree in Law, Olu also enjoys other hobbies, such as reading, dancing, listening to good music, and learning. To Olu, writing is one of the places where she finds true happiness, and she hopes people find happiness in her works too.
